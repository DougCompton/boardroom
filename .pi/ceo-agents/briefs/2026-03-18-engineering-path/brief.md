# Brief

## Situation
There is a decision to make about the next phase of engineering direction. The board should assess whether the path improves leverage, quality, and career or team trajectory.

## Stakes
The wrong path can create technical debt, reduce credibility, or limit future options. The right one can compound expertise and output.

## Constraints
The path must fit current capacity, learning bandwidth, and the need for durable, explainable work.

## Key Question
What engineering path best compounds capability and leverage over the next several years?
