# Brief

## Situation
A strategic buyer has made an acquisition offer. The team needs a fast board recommendation on whether to accept, reject, or negotiate.

## Stakes
Acceptance may crystallize value and reduce risk. Rejection may preserve upside but increases execution risk, financing pressure, and distraction.

## Constraints
The decision window is short. Legal, tax, and diligence work will be required if the offer moves forward. Current operations must continue during review.

## Key Question
Should the company accept the offer as-is, negotiate conditions, or stay independent?
