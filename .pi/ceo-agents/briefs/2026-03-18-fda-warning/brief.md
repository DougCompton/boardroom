# Brief

## Situation
There is an FDA warning or regulatory issue requiring a board response. The question is how to respond without compounding legal, operational, or reputational risk.

## Stakes
The downside includes enforcement, product disruption, and trust damage. The upside is faster correction and a stronger compliance posture.

## Constraints
The response must be accurate, careful, and compatible with legal and regulatory review. No speculation should be treated as fact.

## Key Question
What is the safest and most effective response plan to the warning?
