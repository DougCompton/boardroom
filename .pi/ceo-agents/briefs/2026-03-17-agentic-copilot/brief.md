# Brief

## Situation
The team is evaluating an agentic copilot concept. The board needs to determine whether it should be built, how it should behave, and what it would actually improve.

## Stakes
A strong design could compound engineering output and reuse. A weak design could become fragile automation with hidden failure modes.

## Constraints
The copilot must be understandable, resumable, and auditable. It should fit the existing tool and session model rather than introduce hidden state.

## Key Question
Should the copilot be built, and if so, what narrow job should it own first?
