# Brief

## Situation
A solo plugin is being considered as a focused product bet. The board needs to judge whether it has enough value and distribution potential to justify building.

## Stakes
Success could create a reusable product asset with low overhead. Failure could consume time without creating durable leverage.

## Constraints
The bet must stay small, testable, and consistent with limited solo-builder capacity.

## Key Question
Is this a disciplined solo bet with asymmetric upside, or a distraction disguised as strategy?
