# Brief

## Situation
The team is considering a short-form content platform as a growth bet. The board needs to decide whether the idea deserves investment and a launch plan.

## Stakes
The upside is new audience reach, distribution leverage, and a possible new product line. The downside is distraction, weak retention, and a noisy market with low moat potential.

## Constraints
Time and budget are limited. The product must avoid becoming a one-off content experiment with no reuse across the broader system.

## Key Question
Is this platform a real strategic wedge, or just another expensive channel experiment?
