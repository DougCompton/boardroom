# Brief

## Situation
A home purchase is being considered in the context of working from home. The board must judge whether the move improves long-term stability or adds avoidable friction.

## Stakes
The decision affects cash flow, optionality, lifestyle fit, and the ability to sustain focused work. A bad choice can create a long-duration financial drag.

## Constraints
The analysis must respect budget, financing terms, commute constraints, and the practical requirements of remote work.

## Key Question
Does buying this home strengthen the operating system of life and work, or make it less resilient?
